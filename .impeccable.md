# Design Context

## Audience

TimeSage is for freelancers and small independent teams who track billable time, prepare invoices, and need a calm local workspace they can trust.

## Product Direction

- Private, local-first time tracking and invoicing.
- Data stays on the user's machine.
- The app should feel dependable during repeated daily use.
- Workflows should stay fast: start timer, review time, invoice, export.

## Brand Personality

- Sharper cyber, not neon decoration.
- Graphite surfaces with precise borders and rare electric green or amber accents.
- Professional app density: compact enough for real work, never cramped.
- Flagship quality: polished states, clear hierarchy, and no generic dashboard gloss.

## Accessibility

- Neurodivergent-friendly and dyslexia-aware.
- Preserve light, dark, and high-contrast themes.
- Preserve density controls, font-size controls, reduced-motion support, and keyboard navigation.
- Labels, focus states, empty states, and feedback must stay clear and direct.
